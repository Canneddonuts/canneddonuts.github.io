2d platformer prototype/Macaroon's Misadventure

Arrow keys to move and Z to jump +/- to zoom in/out, IJKL to move the camera.

Hit the P key to toggle the ability to place blocks.

Hit backspace to delete the last block you placed.

Use the 0 or 1 keys to set the type of block.

Hit Q to toggle gravity.

While the gravity is off use 'V' and 'B' to adjust Y velocity.
